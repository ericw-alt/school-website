Self-sustaining

Through the book, Daniel Keyes also conveys that people with intellectual disabilities can live by themselves without too much difficulty. When Charlie was living on his own, he suffers some emotional trauma, and occasionally throws things across the room. However, he was still perfectly capable of supporting himself and caring for his own needs.
