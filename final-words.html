The purpose of this website

This novel shows that people with intellectual disabilities have the capabilities to do many things that ordinary people can do, such as getting a job and succeeding at it, living alone, and even showing emotional concern. In this novel, the main character, Charlie Gordon, is presented as a 32-year-old man suffering from an intellectual disability. As a result, his IQ is only 68, and he can't do many things, such as reading and writing properly.
