What does this website do?

This website was created to thoughtfully explore the novel Flowers for Algernon. This novel has many different aspects that can be deeply explored, such as the plot line, the character development, conflicts, and even the setting. However, this website will only explore one aspect: how book brings out understanding for mentally disabled and challenged people.

About the author

The author of the book Flowers for Algernon is Daniel Keyes. He was born on August 9, 1927 in Brooklyn, New York City, New York, United States and died at age 86 on June 15, 2014 in Boca Raton, Florida, United States because of complications due to pneumonia.

He has written many books such as Flowers for Algernon, The Touch, The Fifth Sally, and The Minds of Billy Milligan.
